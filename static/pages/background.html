The Greater Manchester Dementia Research centre exists as a self-funding entity within Greater Manchester Mental Health Foundation Trust (GMMH). Formally set up in 2018, the centre is concerned with running clinical trials on the NIHR portfolio. The research centre also aims to improve the lives of people living with dementia in GM by democratising access to data. The dashboard is an attempt to collate all of the information on dementia care and related topics in GM. The dashboard is one project within the GMDRC.
